About Bi.P.C

Bi.P.C (Biology, Physics, and Chemistry) is a popular stream in India for students interested in biology, life sciences, and healthcare. It is a common choice for students aiming for careers in medicine, paramedical fields, or life sciences. What is Bi.P.C?

BiPC is the combination of Biology, Physics, and Chemistry in the intermediate or 10+2 level of education. Unlike the MPC (Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry) stream, which is more oriented toward engineering and technology, BiPC is tailored for students interested in medical and life sciences. With this background, students can pursue various undergraduate degrees related to biology, healthcare, and scientific research.

Why Choose Bi.P.C?

BiPC is suitable for students who:

  • Are interested in biological sciences and medicine.
  • Wish to pursue careers in healthcare, research, or allied sciences.
  • Prefer studying living organisms, anatomy, and ecosystems over technical fields like engineering or mathematics.
  • Aspire to work in fields that contribute to health and welfare, research, or environmental conservation.
