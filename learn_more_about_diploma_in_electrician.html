Diploma in Electrician

Comprehensive training in electrical systems and installation techniques

About

A diploma in Electrician studies equips students with practical and theoretical knowledge in electrical wiring, power systems, and maintenance. The program prepares students for careers in electrical installation, repair, and industrial maintenance.

Key Components

  • Electrical Wiring: Basics of residential and commercial wiring.
  • Power Systems: Understanding circuits, transformers, and electrical distribution.
  • Safety Regulations: Training in electrical safety protocols and compliance.
  • Electrical Maintenance: Troubleshooting and repairing electrical systems.
  • Renewable Energy: Basics of solar and wind energy installations.

Practical Training

  • Internships with electrical contractors and industrial plants.
  • Hands-on training in wiring, circuit design, and panel installation.
  • Workshops on power distribution and safety management.
