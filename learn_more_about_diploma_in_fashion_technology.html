Diploma in Fashion Technology

Comprehensive training in fashion design, textiles, and garment production

About

A diploma in Fashion Technology provides students with knowledge and skills in fashion design, textile science, garment manufacturing, and fashion business management. This program prepares students for careers in the fashion industry.

Key Components

  • Fashion Design: Basics of sketching, pattern making, and garment construction.
  • Textile Science: Understanding fabrics, dyeing techniques, and material selection.
  • Apparel Production: Techniques in stitching, cutting, and quality control.
  • Fashion Marketing: Branding, merchandising, and retail management.
  • Trend Analysis: Studying global fashion trends and forecasting styles.

Practical Training

  • Internships with fashion houses, boutiques, or textile industries.
  • Hands-on training in fashion studios and production units.
  • Participation in fashion shows and exhibitions.

Career Opportunities

  • Fashion Designer
  • Textile Designer
  • Apparel Production Manager
  • Fashion Merchandiser
  • Stylist / Costume Designer
