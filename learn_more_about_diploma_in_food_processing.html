Diploma in Food Processing

Comprehensive training in food production, safety, and technology

About

A diploma in Food Processing provides students with in-depth knowledge of food production, preservation, quality control, and safety regulations. The program prepares students for careers in the food and beverage industry.

Key Components

  • Food Science: Understanding food composition, nutrition, and chemistry.
  • Processing Techniques: Methods of food preservation, packaging, and storage.
  • Quality Control: Ensuring food safety standards and hygiene practices.
  • Food Microbiology: Studying microorganisms and their impact on food safety.
  • Supply Chain Management: Logistics and distribution in the food industry.

Practical Training

  • Internships in food production units, quality control labs, and packaging industries.
  • Hands-on training in food testing, processing machinery, and safety protocols.
  • Workshops on food preservation techniques and regulatory compliance.

Career Opportunities

  • Food Processing Technician
  • Quality Control Analyst
  • Food Safety Officer
  • Production Supervisor
  • Food Product Developer
