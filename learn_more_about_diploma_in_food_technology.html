Diploma in Food Technology

Comprehensive training in food science, processing, and safety

About

A diploma in Food Technology provides students with knowledge of food production, preservation, quality control, and safety regulations. The program focuses on modern food processing techniques and technological advancements in the industry.

Key Components

  • Food Chemistry: Understanding the composition and properties of food substances.
  • Food Processing Techniques: Methods of preserving and enhancing food quality.
  • Quality Control and Safety: Ensuring food safety standards and regulations.
  • Nutrition and Dietetics: Understanding nutritional values and dietary planning.
  • Food Microbiology: Study of microorganisms affecting food safety and preservation.

Practical Training

  • Internships in food processing companies and quality control laboratories.
  • Hands-on experience in food testing and analysis.
  • Workshops on food safety regulations and hygiene practices.
