Diploma in International Business

Comprehensive training in global trade, business strategies, and international markets

About

A diploma in International Business equips students with knowledge of global trade, business regulations, and international marketing strategies. The program prepares individuals for careers in multinational corporations and trade organizations.

Key Components

  • Global Trade: Understanding import/export regulations and trade agreements.
  • International Marketing: Strategies for promoting products in global markets.
  • Business Negotiations: Techniques for successful cross-border deals.
  • Foreign Exchange & Finance: Managing currency risks and international transactions.
  • Cross-Cultural Management: Navigating cultural differences in business operations.

Practical Training

  • Internships in multinational companies and trade organizations.
  • Case studies on international business strategies.
  • Workshops on global market analysis and business ethics.

  • Global Marketing Executive
  • Export-Import Coordinator
  • Business Development Manager
