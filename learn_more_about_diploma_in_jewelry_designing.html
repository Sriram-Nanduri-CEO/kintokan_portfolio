Diploma in Jewelry Designing

Comprehensive training in jewelry design, craftsmanship, and industry trends

About

A diploma in Jewelry Designing provides students with artistic and technical skills in designing jewelry. The program covers design principles, material knowledge, and the use of specialized tools and software for creating stunning jewelry pieces.

Key Components

  • Design Principles: Fundamentals of aesthetics, sketching, and 3D modeling.
  • Material Knowledge: Understanding metals, gemstones, and other materials used in jewelry.
  • Jewelry Manufacturing: Techniques like casting, engraving, and stone setting.
  • Computer-Aided Design (CAD): Digital tools for creating precise jewelry designs.
  • Market Trends: Insights into the jewelry industry, branding, and customer preferences.

Practical Training

  • Hands-on experience in jewelry workshops and studios.
  • Internships with jewelry brands, designers, and manufacturers.
  • Portfolio development and exhibition of designed pieces.

Career Opportunities

  • Jewelry Designer
  • Gemologist
  • CAD Designer for Jewelry
  • Jewelry Merchandiser
  • Entrepreneur / Jewelry Business Owner
