Diploma in Marine Engineering

Comprehensive training in marine technology, ship mechanics, and naval systems

About

A diploma in Marine Engineering prepares students for careers in the maritime industry by providing knowledge of ship operations, marine machinery, and safety regulations. The program focuses on technical skills required for the maintenance and operation of marine vessels.

Key Components

  • Ship Mechanics: Understanding engine systems, propulsion, and maintenance.
  • Naval Architecture: Basics of ship design, structure, and stability.
  • Marine Safety: Training on emergency procedures, firefighting, and environmental regulations.
  • Electrical and Control Systems: Marine electrical circuits, automation, and power management.
  • Fluid Mechanics and Thermodynamics: Principles of heat transfer and fluid dynamics in marine applications.

Practical Training

  • Internships on ships, docks, and maritime companies.
  • Hands-on training with marine engines and propulsion systems.
  • Workshops on safety protocols, ship maintenance, and navigation technology.

Career Opportunities

  • Ship Maintenance Technician
  • Naval Architect Assistant
  • Marine Safety Officer
