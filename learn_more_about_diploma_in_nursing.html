Diploma in Nursing

Comprehensive training in patient care and medical assistance

About

A diploma in Nursing prepares students for a career in healthcare, focusing on patient care, medical ethics, and clinical skills. This program equips students with theoretical knowledge and practical experience needed to work in hospitals, clinics, and healthcare settings.

Key Components

  • Fundamentals of Nursing: Basic nursing principles, patient care, and hygiene.
  • Medical-Surgical Nursing: Understanding diseases, treatments, and surgical procedures.
  • Pharmacology: Administration of medications and drug safety.
  • Community Health Nursing: Public health, preventive care, and health education.
  • Emergency and Critical Care: Handling emergencies and intensive care unit procedures.

Practical Training

  • Clinical rotations in hospitals, clinics, and nursing homes.
  • Hands-on training in patient care, first aid, and medical procedures.
  • Workshops on infection control, wound care, and medical ethics.

Career Opportunities

  • Registered Nurse
  • Hospital Nurse
  • Community Health Worker
  • Emergency Care Assistant
  • Home Healthcare Nurse
