Diploma in Photography

Comprehensive training in photography techniques and creative skills

About

A diploma in Photography equips students with technical and artistic skills in capturing, editing, and presenting images. It prepares individuals for careers in photography, media, and creative industries.

Key Components

  • Camera Techniques: Understanding camera settings, exposure, and composition.
  • Lighting and Studio Work: Using natural and artificial lighting effectively.
  • Photo Editing: Post-processing with software like Photoshop and Lightroom.
  • Commercial Photography: Product, fashion, and event photography techniques.
  • Visual Storytelling: Creating compelling narratives through photography.

Practical Training

  • Workshops and hands-on assignments in various photography styles.
  • Internships with professional photographers, studios, or media houses.
  • Portfolio development and exhibitions to showcase work.

Career Opportunities

  • Professional Photographer
  • Photojournalist
  • Commercial Photographer
  • Studio Photographer
  • Freelance Photographer
