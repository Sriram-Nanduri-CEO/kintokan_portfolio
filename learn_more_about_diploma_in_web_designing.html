Diploma in Web Designing

Comprehensive training in web development and design principles

About

A diploma in Web Designing provides students with essential skills in designing and developing websites. It covers front-end and back-end technologies, UX/UI principles, and responsive design techniques.

Key Components

  • HTML, CSS, and JavaScript: Fundamentals of web structure, styling, and interactivity.
  • Responsive Design: Creating mobile-friendly and adaptive websites.
  • Graphic Design and UX/UI: Principles of user experience, wireframing, and design tools.
  • Front-end Frameworks: Learning Bootstrap, React, or other popular frameworks.
  • Basic Back-end Development: Introduction to databases, PHP, or Node.js.

Practical Training

  • Internships in web development firms or freelance projects.
  • Hands-on experience with industry tools like Figma, Adobe XD, and WordPress.
  • Live projects and website development to build a professional portfolio.
