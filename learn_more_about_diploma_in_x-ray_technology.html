Diploma in X-ray Technology

Comprehensive training in radiographic imaging and diagnostic procedures

About

A diploma in X-ray Technology provides students with knowledge of radiographic imaging techniques, patient care, and safety procedures. This program prepares individuals for careers in medical imaging and diagnostic radiology.

Key Components

  • Radiographic Techniques: Understanding X-ray equipment, imaging methods, and radiation safety.
  • Human Anatomy and Physiology: Studying the structure and functions of the human body.
  • Medical Imaging Procedures: Performing diagnostic imaging and assisting radiologists.
  • Radiation Protection: Ensuring patient and technician safety during imaging procedures.
  • Pathology: Understanding diseases and their radiographic appearances.

Practical Training

  • Internships in hospitals, diagnostic centers, or radiology labs.
  • Hands-on experience with X-ray machines and imaging software.
  • Practical training in patient positioning and radiation safety protocols.

Career Opportunities

  • X-ray Technologist
  • Medical Imaging Assistant
  • Diagnostic Radiographer
